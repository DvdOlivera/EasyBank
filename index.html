Who we are


We are building the world’s first truly global financial superapp. In 2015, Easybank launched in the UK offering money transfer and exchange. Today, our customers around the world use dozens of Easybank’s innovative products to make more than 100 million transactions a month.

Across our personal and business accounts, we help customers improve their financial health, give them more control, and connect people seamlessly across the world.
